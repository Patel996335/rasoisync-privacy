Privacy Policy

App: RasoiSync  |  Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during onboarding (household size, dietary preferences, cooking style) and optional account information (email address for sign-in). We do not collect your name, payment details, or government identifiers.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your dietary and household preferences are used solely to generate personalized weekly meal plans. Your email, if provided, is used only for authentication (magic link sign-in). We do not use your data for advertising.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL), hosted on AWS infrastructure in the United States. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase – database and authentication (privacy policy)
  • OpenAI – meal plan generation (privacy policy). We send anonymized dietary preferences only — no personal identifiers.
  • RevenueCat – subscription management (privacy policy)

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time by emailing us and we will remove your data within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

RasoiSync is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions? Email us at support@rasoisync.com
